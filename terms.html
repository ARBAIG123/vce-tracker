2. What VCE Tracker Does

VCE Tracker is a browser-based tool that helps Vardhaman College of Engineering students track their attendance percentages, monitor subject-wise CGPA, calculate safe bunk counts, manually log daily attendance, and predict semester GPA using a grade calculator. All data processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to or stored on any external server operated by VCE Tracker.

3. How the Bookmarklet Works

The VCE Tracker bookmarklet is a small piece of JavaScript code saved in your browser. When clicked while you are logged into the official VCE student portal at student.vardhaman.org, it reads your attendance and CGPA data from the page using your existing authenticated session. It does not store, transmit, or share your login credentials. Your username and password are never accessed or seen by VCE Tracker at any point.
